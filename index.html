Arshad Nadeem

Arshad Nadeem, a Pakistani athlete, is known for his achievements in javelin throw, including his participation in the 2020 Tokyo Olympics. He became the first Pakistani athlete to qualify for the final of an Olympic track and field event, finishing 5th with a throw of 84.62 meters. Nadeem has also won medals in Commonwealth Games and Asian Games. Born in Punjab, Pakistan, his success has inspired many young athletes, highlighting the country's potential in athletics.
